SENSORY GYM

We designed our sensory gym to provide each child with individualized treatment specifically for your child's needs. Our focus is to help your child tolerate and integrate sensory input through playful activities. The gym provides each child the opportunity to receive sensory input to enhance their development and to promote organization, sequencing, attention, endurance and strength.

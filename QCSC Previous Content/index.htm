QUALITY SERVICES from CARING THERAPISTS

Quality Care Speech Center is a pediatric Speech and Language, Occupational and Physical Therapy Center serving children of all ages. Quality Care Speech Center was founded in 2004 by Randi Pino, a Speech-Language Pathologist. Mrs. Pino went on to hire other caring and dedicated Speech-Language Pathologists like herself in addition to Occupational and Physical Therapists. Quality Care Speech Center currently employs 20 therapists and that number is continuously growing. Every therapist employed by Quality Care Speech Center is NYS Licensed. Our Speech Pathologists are ASHA certified and hold a Teacher of Speech and Hearing Handicapped License.

At Quality Care Speech Center your child is our main priority. Excellent Speech-Language Pathologists, Occupational and Physical Therapists and a top notch administrative staff join together to provide the highest quality care for you and your family. We are committed to providing each patient with a treatment plan that includes not only exceptional care, but extraordinary results. Children are seen in our private treatment rooms to ensure maximum acquisition of material with minimal distractions. Each room is child friendly and color schemed to enhance learning. After every session therapists report to parents and/or guardians to ensure carryover of material learned and reinforcement of new skills taught. Our facility has an abundance of educational toys, games, books and other materials to keep your child focused and interested in each of their sessions from start to finish. Our children are always rewarded for good work during and after each therapy session with verbal praise and their token of choice. We find that our clients love coming to therapy. Excellent rapport between child and therapist and a comfortable waiting area for our parents to relax, read and listen to soft music while waiting for their child keeps our clientele happy and looking forward to services.

Our main facility is located in Staten Island, NY and we offer homecare services throughout both Staten Island and New York City. In addition to homecare we also offer services at your child’s school and/or daycare center. Our Staten Island facility is open Monday thru Friday from 8:30am – 8pm to suit our early risers and working parents. Our administrative staff works to accommodate you. We understand that children have other activities and life is busy, so we put 100% effort into working with you to create the best schedule for you and your child.

Quality Care Speech Center is an approved Department of Education Provider for preschool (CPSE) and school age (RSA) children. For children not approved to receive services through the Department of Education we offer private pay options with very competitive rates.
